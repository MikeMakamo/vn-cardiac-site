About the Founder

Meet Veli Ntshangase

An accomplished Diagnostic Cardiac Technologist who graduated with distinction from Durban University of Technology. Veli's journey was deeply personal — inspired by witnessing his grandfather's struggle with undiagnosed heart disease, he dedicated his career to ensuring communities have access to quality cardiac care.

After years of hands-on training and mastering diagnostic machines, Veli founded VN Cardiac Diagnostics to bring mobile cardiac services directly to underserved areas of Southern KwaZulu-Natal.
